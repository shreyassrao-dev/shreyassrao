Hey there! Meet Shreyass Rao.

“As wholesome as an idli
as awesome as BengaLuru”

Shreyass Rao, Bengaluru’s idli-loving, filter coffee-sipping creative dynamo, is a comedian, actor and content creator with a burst of creativity and zest wrapped as a human parcel. You’ll often find him whipping up killer content or shooting ads that pop like perfectly steamed idlis. 

His creativity? As robust as a strong cup of filter coffee. His ideas? As fluffy and fresh as idlis straight off the steamer.

Shreyass thrives on fresh ideas that feel like a breath of fresh air amid the bustling world of Instagram. With a knack for shooting captivating ads, he’s always on the lookout for new opportunities to channel his inner artist.

From savoring every bite of his favorite South Indian delicacy to crafting engaging content that stands out, Shreyass is dedicated to making the world a little brighter and a little more enjoyable, one video at a time!

Where are the idlis served?

Shreyass dishes out his delightful content to his followers on social media. 
Follow his quirky adventures and creative exploits on Instagram, Facebook and X!
